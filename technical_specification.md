# Landing page No. 3 — Local business (Restaurant)

## General information

- **Niche:** Restaurant business / HoReCa
- **The client:** Ember Restaurant — steakhouse in New York
- **The purpose of the website:** Book a table online + increase awareness
- **Target audience:** Wealthy people aged 30-55, business meetings and romantic dinners
- **Domain (demo):** ember-steakhouse.vercel.app

## Section structure

### 1. Navbar

- The logo
- Navigation: Menu / Story / Reservations / Events
- Button: "Reserve a Table"

### 2. Hero (Full-screen)

- Full-screen video or photo: steak on coals, smoke, beautiful
  - Small text: `EST. 2019 · NEW YORK CITY'
- Large headline: **"EMBER"**
- Subtitle: *"Where fire meets flavor"*
  - Button: "Reserve Your Table"

### 3. About the restaurant (Story)

- Headline: "A Story Told Through Fire"
- Text: the history of the restaurant, the chef's philosophy, the use of only local products
- Small facts: "Since 2019" / "14 Awards" / "Chef Marco Rodriguez"

### 4. Menu Highlights

- Title: "Signature Dishes"
- 3-4 cards with photos of the dish, name, description, price:
- Ribeye 16oz — dry-aged, truffle butter — $89
  - Wagyu Tenderloin — Japanese A5 — $145
  - Lobster Bisque — starter — $28
  - Lava Cake — dessert — $18
- The "View Full Menu" button

### 5. Ambiance

- Photo gallery: interior, bar, dishes, guests
- Headline: "The Experience"
- A short text about the atmosphere

### 6. Special Events (Private Events)

- Title: "Host Your Next Event"
- Description: corporate parties, birthdays, weddings
- Button: "Enquire About Events"

### 7. Reviews

- 3 quotes from the guests
- Rating

### 8. Reservation Form

- Headline: "Reserve Your Table"
- Form: Name / Email / Phone / Date / Time / Number of guests / Special requests
- Button: "Confirm Reservation"
- Under the form: "Or call us: +1 (212) 555-0198"

###9. Contacts

- Address, opening hours, phone number

### 10. Footer

- The logo
  Facebook Instagram, social media (Navigation)
- "Crafted with passion in New York City"

## Technical requirements

- Adaptability: required
- Navbar
- Booking form: validation of fields + success message
- Stack: Next.js + Tailwind CSS
- Deployment: Vercel
